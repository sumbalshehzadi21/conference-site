We are delighted to announce 8th Two Days International Conference on “Global Halal Food Industry: Insights from Pakistan” being organized by Institute of Islamic Studies, Islamic Research Center, Institute of Sufism and Mysticism and the Faculty of Food Science & Nutrition Bahauddin Zakariya University (BZU), Multan-Pakistan on April 24th and 25th, 2024, at Jinnah Auditorium, BZU, Multan in collaboration with The International Islamic University (IIU) Islamabad, Pakistan.

Objectives

Objectives of the Conference:

Gain an in-depth understanding of Islamic rulings related to Halāl and Harām food. Explore practical Islamic guidelines focusing on modern issues such as E-Numbers, Gelatine, Carmine, L-Cysteine, and Glycerine.

Delve into topics like Enzymes, Alcohol, Ethanol, Emulsifiers, Acidity regulators, genetically modified foods (GMF), the use of stunning in slaughtering, Harām Medicine, and Halāl certification.

Discuss Islamic narratives on some of the challenging concepts such as hygiene, animal welfare and food quality.

In order to create a global network of support and resources for the future, provide a forum and opportunities for interpersonal interaction between scholars of Shari'ah, professionals in the food sector, researchers, and those involved with halal certification authorities.

To explore the role of Halāl Food Industry in sustainable Development and economic growth in Pakistan.

Examine Islamic perspectives on contemporary challenges in food production and consumption. Discuss ethical considerations and practical applications within the context of Halāl food practices
